Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a beloved Indian dish known for its rich, creamy tomato-based sauce and tender pieces of marinated chicken.

Ingredients

  • Indian Spices
  • Marinade Chicken Breast
  • Clove Garlic
  • Heavy Cream
  • Tomatoes
  • Chopped Fresh Cliantro

How to make Chicken Tikka Masala

  1. Marinate the Chicken
  2. Cook the Chicken
  3. Prepare the Sauce
  4. Put spice and tomatoes
  5. Season and Serve
