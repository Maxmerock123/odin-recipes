Tom Yam Kung Recipe

Tom Yam Kung: Thai spicy soup

Tom Yum Kung, a famous Thai soup, is another delightful dish to explore.

Ingredients

  • Large shrimp
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Lime leaves
  • Ginger
  • Thai bird's eye chillies
  • Sliced muchrooms
  • Onion
  • Fish Sauce

How to make Tom Yam Kung

  1. Prepare the broth
  2. Add Vegetables
  3. Season the soup
  4. Cook the shrimp
  5. Season and Serve
